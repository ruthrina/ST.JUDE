Educational Support: A Lifeline for Children’s Future

At St. Thadeous Charity, we believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating opportunities for a brighter future. Many children and orphans lack access to quality education due to financial struggles, lack of resources, and limited guidance. Our Educational Support Program provides the necessary tools and assistance to ensure these children get the education they deserve.

Learn More

How We Support Education

Scholarships & Bursaries

We provide full and partial scholarships for students in primary, secondary, and vocational schools.

  • Full & partial scholarships.
  • Emergency bursaries.
  • Partnerships with schools for reduced fees.

Tutoring & Mentorship

We help struggling students through tutoring, mentorship, and study groups.

  • After-school tutoring.
  • Mentorship programs with professionals.
  • Study groups & online learning.

School Supplies & Learning Materials

We provide necessary school materials for children in need.

  • Stationery packs (notebooks, pens, rulers).
  • School uniforms & shoes.
  • Textbooks & digital resources.

Vocational Training & Skill Development

We support students who wish to pursue skill-based careers.

  • Training in carpentry, tailoring, computing.
  • Entrepreneurship workshops.
  • Internship placements for experience.

Why Educational Support Matters

Reduces Poverty

A child with a solid education has more opportunities for a better future.

Builds Confidence

Learning new skills empowers children to believe in themselves and succeed.

Strengthens Communities

Educated individuals create positive change in their communities, benefiting all.
