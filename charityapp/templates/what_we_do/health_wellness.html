Promoting Health, Empowering Lives

At ST.THADDAEUS CHILDREN FOUNDATION LIMITED, we believe that good health is the foundation for a fulfilling life. Our approach emphasizes the connection between physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. By providing resources, education, and support, we help individuals lead healthier, more balanced lives. Through our holistic health initiatives, we strive to promote physical wellness, mental peace, and spiritual growth, enabling individuals to live their lives to the fullest and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Health and Wellness Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to promote physical health, mental well-being, and community outreach. Each program is tailored to meet the needs of individuals and families in our community. Here are the programs we offer:

Medical Assistance & Health Screenings

Offering medical check-ups, basic healthcare services, and screenings to underserved communities. We aim to improve access to essential healthcare services for those in need.

Mental Health Support

Providing counseling, workshops, and resources for individuals dealing with mental health issues such as anxiety, depression, and stress. Our program seeks to promote mental well-being and resilience.

Nutrition & Healthy Eating

Educating and providing resources on maintaining a healthy diet. We offer food assistance for families in need and guide healthier eating choices for a better life.

Fitness & Exercise

Organizing fitness classes, wellness challenges, and exercise groups to encourage physical activity. Our goal is to improve overall health through active living.

Health Education & Awareness

Offering classes, workshops, and outreach programs to raise awareness about various health issues. We provide preventive care tips to help individuals take charge of their health.
