Welcome to ST.THADDAEUS CHILDREN FOUNDATION LIMITED

At ST.Thaddeus Children Foundation Limited, we are dedicated to transforming the lives of needy children and orphans by ensuring they receive education, spiritual guidance, and moral upbringing. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to grow in a supportive and nurturing environment, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our commitment extends beyond just providing for their immediate needs, as we aim to empower them to achieve a brighter future and create lasting positive change in their communities.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To nurture an educated, spiritually, and morally well-groomed generation.

Mission

To help needy children and orphans attain education while fostering spiritual and moral values.

A Message from Our Founder, Rev.Fr.Kyekulidde Mubiru Jude

Through this charity, we aim to offer not just education, but a holistic development that builds both spiritual and moral strength. Every child deserves the opportunity to succeed, and it is our calling to help them realize their full potential.

I invite you to join us in this noble cause and help us create a brighter, more hopeful future for children in need. Together, we can make an enduring impact and change lives for the better.

Rev.Fr.Kyekulidde Mubiru Jude

History & Background

ST.THADDAEUS CHILDREN FOUNDATION LIMITED was founded in 2017 at St. Charles Lwanga Catholic Church Mutundwe by Rev. Fr. Thaddaeus Kyekulidde Mubiru. It started with the vision of helping needy children and seminarians who cannot afford basic school requirements such as books, pens, uniforms, and school fees.

Rev. Fr. Jude's inspiration came when his mother passed away while he was in primary one, following the passing of his father when he was just two years old. He received help from the elders of his village who solicited books, pens, pencils, and school fees for him, enabling him to continue his education.

From that experience, Rev. Fr. Jude set a goal to create avenues for assisting and helping needy and orphaned children to ensure they receive an education.

The fund takes on and supports children from various denominations, from primary, secondary schools and universities.
